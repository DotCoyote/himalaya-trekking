Welcome to Himalaya Trekking!

My name is Santosh, and I can guide you!

I am a professional trekking guide with over 30 years of experience. I have been guiding trekkers from all over the world to the Himalayas. I have a deep knowledge of the mountains, the culture, and the people of the Himalayas. I can help you plan your trek, provide you with all the information you need, and guide you safely through the mountains.

Whether you are a seasoned trekker or a first-time visitor to the Himalayas, I can help you have an unforgettable experience. I offer a range of treks, from short day hikes to challenging high-altitude treks. I can also help you plan a custom trek that suits your interests, fitness level, and schedule.

I am committed to providing you with a safe, enjoyable, and memorable trekking experience. I am passionate about the mountains and the people who live there, and I want to share that passion with you. I will do everything I can to ensure that you have a great time in the Himalayas and that you leave with wonderful memories that will last a lifetime.
